What if I need help?

keyboard_arrow_down

If you are experiencing severe distress, please contact a crisis support service, call 911, or visit your local emergency department for immediate assistance. These services are available 24/7 to provide urgent support. In Canada, the Crisis helpline can be accessed by calling or texting 988. Additional community resources specific to your province can be found here. During difficult times, it's important to take steps to care for your mental health. Please remember that there are resources available to provide the support you need, and reaching out can be an essential part of the process.
