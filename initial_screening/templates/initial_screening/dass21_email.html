A total score above 60 indicate the client is experiencing Severe distress.

STEP Solo: As a reminder, those with severe symptoms of distress (total scores above 60) or severe symptoms of depression (depression scores of 21 or greater) are not recommended to participate in STEP Solo. Instead, the clinician may prefer to evaluate if STEP Together or one-to-one interventions would be most appropriate for these individuals.

STEP Together: As a reminder, there are no cut-off scores on the DASS-21 for participation in a STEP Together group. These scores are provided to monitor participant progress.
